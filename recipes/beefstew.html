Dinty Moore canned Beef Stew

It's literally just a can of beef stew, it's a stew with beef in it.

So I could list some of the ingredients that are listed on the can, but I would have to go back to my kitchen and get the can back out of the cabinet and I really don't wanna do that. Just go to the store, and buy a can of beef stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Dinty Moore Beef Stew
  1. Empty stew into microwave-safe bowl
  2. Cover
  3. Heat on HIGH (100% power) 3 minutes or until hot, stirring after 2 minutes
  4. Stir before serving
