Great Value Chili with Beans

Chili with beans in it, it's not great, but it's cheap and works when you're too lazy to go to the store.

So I could list some of the ingredients that are listed on the can, but I would have to go back to my kitchen and get the can back out of the cabinet and I really don't wanna do that. Just go to the store, and buy a can of chili. I will say though, if you want the Great Value brand, you're gonna have to go to Walmart specifically.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Great Value Chili with Beans
  1. Empty chili into microwave-safe bowl
  2. Cover loosely
  3. Heat on high (100% power) 3 minutes or until hot, stirring once after 2 minutes
  4. Stir before serving
